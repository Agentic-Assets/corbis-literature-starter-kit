---
description: Design or stress-test an identification strategy
---

Run the `finance-identification-design` skill (and `real-estate-empirical-design` if the paper involves property markets, mortgages, or spatial data).

$ARGUMENTS

Produce:
1. Estimand in plain English and notation
2. Source of variation and why it is plausibly exogenous
3. Baseline specification in notation and code sketch
4. Threat map: every identified threat matched to a diagnostic or remedy
5. Modern methods check (staggered DiD, Roth pretrends, Oster bounds, AAIW clustering)
6. Diagnostics and falsification battery
7. Mechanism and heterogeneity test plan
8. What the design cannot claim

Use `search_papers` to find published papers using comparable designs for precedent.
